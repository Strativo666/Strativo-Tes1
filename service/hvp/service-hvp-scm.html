Pendekatan STRATIVO: Integrated Supply Chain Excellence

1. Supply Chain Diagnostic & Network Design

Audit komprehensif menggunakan framework SCOR (Supply Chain Operations Reference). Pemetaan end-to-end dari supplier hingga customer, identifikasi bottleneck, dan perancangan network optimal untuk efisiensi maksimal.

2. Inventory Optimization & Demand Planning

Implementasi sistem inventory management berbasis data: ABC analysis, safety stock calculation, demand forecasting akurat, dan automated reorder point untuk menghindari overstock & stockout.

3. Supplier Relationship & Procurement Excellence

Strategic sourcing, supplier evaluation & development, contract negotiation, dan procurement process optimization untuk mendapatkan harga terbaik dengan kualitas terjamin.

4. Distribution & Logistics Optimization

Route optimization, warehouse layout redesign, transportation management, dan last-mile delivery strategy untuk mempercepat delivery dan menekan biaya distribusi.

Alur Kerja Kami

01

Supply Chain Diagnostic

Audit mendalam terhadap seluruh aspek SCM: procurement, inventory, warehousing, distribution. Kumpulkan baseline metrics dan identifikasi pain points (1 minggu)

02

Network Design & Optimization

Perancangan ulang network supply chain: jumlah & lokasi gudang, supplier selection, transportation mode, dan inventory positioning (1-2 minggu)

03

Process Redesign & System Implementation

Pembuatan SOP baru, implementasi tools (inventory management, demand planning), training tim, dan pilot testing (2-3 minggu)

04

Rollout & Performance Monitoring

Full implementation, coaching tim, monitoring KPI harian/mingguan, continuous improvement, dan scale-up ke seluruh network (30-60 hari)
